Our Leading Fire-Resistant Products

Firefree 88

Firefree 88 ("FF88") is our high-quality, intumescent fire-resistant paint. This non-toxic, thin-film, and water-based product is renowned for its exceptional fire-resistant capabilities, requiring only a thin layer of FF88. The fire resistance capabilities of these coatings are certified and fully tested with multiple standards worldwide.

Tested to global standards such as ASTM, FM, NFPA, UBC, EN, and SISIR, our coatings establish a fire barrier across various materials and assemblies. Our coating has also received official approvals from FM Approvals, undergone inspections by ICC, obtained classification by UL, and acknowledged by NY MEA. These certifications demonstrate the reliability of our fire-resistant coating and show our commitment to adhering to stringent safety protocols. Withstanding temperatures of up to 2000°F for two hours, FF88 offers versatility in application methods and often functions as a self-priming agent, contributing to cost reduction. FF88's environmentally conscious profile, marked by low VOCs and alignment with green standards, showcases its adaptability across a range of substrates in construction and transportation. These coatings come with a two-hour fire rating that enhances their versatility and suitability for external use, with the Firefree Exterior Topcoat ("FfE") broadening their application scope across diverse environments.

Firefree Class A

Firefree Class A, or FfA, is our high-quality flame-retardant paint. It's safe, water-based, and meets all the necessary safety standards. This paint is tested to meet ASTM E84 requirements, including the extended 30-minute test. This certification means these coatings meet the industry standard for not catching fire easily in the Wildland Urban Interface.

Compliant with all ASTM E-84 requirements, including the 30-Minute Extended test, these coatings represent a high-quality, nonflammable, non-toxic, thin-film intumescent flame-retardant paint. This flame-retardant paint, inspected by the ICC (International Code Council), is easy to use, featuring a smooth architectural-grade finish and longevity. These eco-friendly coatings possess low VOCs (36gr /liter) compared to regulatory limits, including those set by California state. These coatings are used in residential, multifamily, commercial, and industrial projects and are applicable to various combustible materials, such as OSB, Douglas fir, plywood, and wood paneling. You can easily apply this low-odor coating with a sprayer, brush, or roller. This product demonstrates the absence of substantial progressive combustion even when subjected to a full 30-minute test over a combustible surface. This indicates that these coatings are highly usable on various flammable materials. This underscores its efficacy in preserving the structural integrity of wood in fire emergencies.

Firefree Wildfire System

The Firefree Wildfire System, the first and only intumescent coating, complies with ASTM E2768 and ASTM E84 standards for ignition resistance and application in Wildland-Urban Interface (WUI) fire zones. The system undergoes rigorous ASTM D2898 method B testing for weatherization, proving its resilience to water and UV exposures, making it suitable for exterior use.

The Firefree Wildfire System underwent testing to adhere to the California Building Code (CBC), specifically SFM 12-7A-3 conditions, as outlined in ASTM E 2957. This evaluation focused on the system's fire resistance against wildfire flame penetration for elements like soffits, eaves, and ignition-resistant materials in various assembly projections, including deck structures, cantilevers, and more. It effectively slows horizontal flame spread & smoke development, meeting fire-retardant-treated lumber criteria. This nontoxic coating also meets fire and weatherization standards that assist insurance availability and rates. Moreover, the Firefree System comprises two coatings: Firefree Class A for ignition resistance and Firefree Exterior topcoat for long-term weather protection, eliminating the need for annual reapplication. Meeting ASTM WUI fire standards, the system passes both fire and weatherization tests, confirmed by CalFire Accredited Fire Test Laboratories & IAS, with ICC-ES listing. It provides a high-quality exterior finish with stain-resistant prime and a durable white satin finish, customizable for color and finish.
